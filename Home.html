Welcome to Madison's Jewels!

Madison’s Jewels is my own personal jewelry line. I started my business one day my freshman year of college. Back in middle school I had taken a jewelry making class. I was curious to see if I was still able to make it, and put it to the test. Turns out yes, and almost two years later my jewelry business is thriving.

Being a fashion major, I love being on the creative side of the spectrum. I am not able to design clothing, accessories, shoes, etc. by drawing them, so I use my creativity by making jewelry. I source my materials internationally. From there, I hand make everything I sell. In the Madison’s Jewels line, I sell earrings, bracelets, necklaces and chokers. Some of the different materials I use is leather, gold plated, silver plated, tassels, natural stones, different metals, and rosary chain.

I love being able to express my creativity and passion for jewelry trends through my own small business. I hope you all enjoy my Jewels!!
